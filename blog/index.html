Initiates Workshop for Students

Apr 1st, 2012

TDCC initiates workshops for students and others who are interested to join the industry or start own enterprises. Workshops for the following fields are being held: Mechanical, Electrical, Electronics & Communications, Electronics & Telecommunications, Software, Biotechnology engineering, Social Sciences, Applied Sciences, Management, Business and Entrepreneurship. Please see the ‘Workshop’ section under ‘Employment’.

Shortlisted by US-India Science & Technology Forum

Aug 2nd, 2011

TDCC shortlisted by United States – India Science and Technology Forum for Development of Low Cost Diagnostic Method for Diabetes Prevention. The method under development in collaboration with Prevendia Inc., California is expected to impact an estimated 68 million non-diabetic non-obese undiagnosed insulin resistant population and 40 million obese insulin resistant population in the US; and an estimated 70-100 million population in India, 50% of whom live in rural areas and 30% in urban areas.
